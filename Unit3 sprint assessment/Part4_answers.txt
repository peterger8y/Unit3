Q2 Answer: Document Stores are ideal in situations where only horizontal scaling is available
and where ACID requirements are not a high priority; basically, where total data authenticity isn't a concern.

Q3 Answer: NewSQL are scalable relational database management systems designed with
horizontal scaling in mind. They try to achieve the best of both NoSQL and SQL databases.
